Top 7 Britney Spears Albums

While all of the songs listed below are amazing works of art, the best Britney Spears song of all time is Toxic. Toxic is great for all occasions, whether you are showering, driving, dancing, being by yourself, or whatever! Slave 4 U is another great Britney song that is often overlooked and uses the super trendy concept of abbreviating words. The music video for this song is also a must see for everyone. It features yet another one of Britney’s many talents: amazing dancing. Chris Cox Megamix is my favorite song to listen to because it is a very cohesive mashup of all of Britney’s top hits. Although it’s not on any of her official produced albums, you can find it on her top hits album.
